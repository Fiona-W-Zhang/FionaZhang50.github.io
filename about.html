    • About Me

    I am a high school student from the Bay Area that is interested in art and computer science. Ever since I was young I have always been a visual artist, drawing, doodling, sketching, and painting, characters and scenery. I enjoyed art as a creative outlet and I have kept art in my life through attending art lessons while working on my own art at the same time. However once I reached high school, I discovered computer science, a new skill and form of language that also allowed me to express myself but in a different way. Computer science provided me with the skills to create new products and my own projects, such as a game program, a calculator, machine learning applications, and a website. Besides art and computer science, I also enjoy reading, playing badminton, and watching movies.
